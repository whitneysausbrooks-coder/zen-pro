🧠 The Neuro-Resilience "Deep Dive"
Since we've initialized the wearable bridge, let's look at how the data actually flows to create that Neuro-Resilience Score. By anchoring on HRV (Heart Rate Variability), we are measuring the balance between the Sympathetic (Stress) and Parasympathetic (Recovery) nervous systems.
The Data Flow:
1.	Ingest: Raw data (HRV, Sleep, Steps) hits your new WearableIntegration service.
2.	Normalize: Your 7-day EMA (Exponential Moving Average) smooths out the "one bad night" noise.
3.	Weight: * HRV (50%): The "Readiness" anchor.
• Sleep (35%): The "Recovery" floor.
• Strain (15%): The "Metabolic" load.
4.	Output: A proprietary score that triggers Corporate Wellness Heatmaps for admins.
