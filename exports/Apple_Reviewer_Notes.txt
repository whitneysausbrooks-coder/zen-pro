WHAT THIS APP DOES
NeuroQuest is a B2B brain-training and wellness app distributed exclusively through enterprise pilot programs. End users are employees of customer companies (currently Kaylee's Creatives and Bead'd) who receive a one-time company invite code from their HR or wellness lead. There is no consumer signup, no self-serve purchase path, and no public registration. We are not a direct-to-consumer health app.

NOTE ON HEALTHKIT TESTING
A real Neuro Resilience Score requires an Apple Watch with recorded HRV, Sleep, and Step data. The review device may have no recorded data, in which case the app shows "No health data found" with an Open Settings option — this is the documented graceful path. If permission is denied, every tab in the app remains fully usable; HealthKit is optional, not required.

HEALTHKIT DATA USAGE
Read-only: Heart Rate Variability (SDNN), Sleep Analysis, Step Count. Never written back to Apple Health. Sent to our server over HTTPS, encrypted at rest, used only to compute the user's individual Neuro Resilience Score. Aggregated for employer dashboards only when 5 or more team members participate (k-anonymity threshold of 5); individual data is never visible to employers under any circumstances. Full HealthKit data handling: https://neuroquestzen.pro/privacy.

ACCOUNT DELETION
Available in-app under Profile → Delete Account. Permanently removes the user record, all HealthKit-derived scores, and all session data from our servers. Compliant with Guideline 5.1.1(v).

PAYMENT MODEL
Companies pay $50 per seat per year directly to NeuroQuest via Stripe (B2B services exempt under Guideline 3.1.3(b)). Employees never see a paywall. The Shop tab sells optional cosmetic upgrades via Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit), as required by Guideline 3.1.1.

PRIVACY AND TRACKING
This app does not use IDFA, does not implement App Tracking Transparency prompts (we do no tracking), and contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We do not read Location, Contacts, Photos, Calendar, or Microphone. HealthKit background delivery is disabled; data is read only when the user opens the Wearable screen and taps Sync.

MEDICAL DISCLAIMER
NeuroQuest is a wellness tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. This is stated in the app footer and on every public-facing page.
