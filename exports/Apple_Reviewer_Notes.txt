NOTE ON HEALTHKIT TESTING
A real Neuro Resilience Score requires an Apple Watch with recorded HRV, Sleep, and Step data. The review device may have no recorded data, in which case the app shows "No health data found" with an Open Settings option — this is the documented graceful path. If permission is denied, every tab remains fully usable; HealthKit is optional, not required.

HEALTHKIT DATA USAGE
Read-only: Heart Rate Variability (SDNN), Sleep Analysis, Step Count. Never written back to Apple Health. Sent over HTTPS, encrypted at rest, used only to compute the user's Neuro Resilience Score. Aggregated for employer dashboards only when 5 or more team members participate (k-anonymity threshold of 5); individual data is never visible to employers. Full details: https://neuroquestzen.pro/privacy.

ACCOUNT DELETION
Available in-app under Profile → Delete Account. Permanently removes the user record, all HealthKit-derived scores, and all session data. Compliant with Guideline 5.1.1(v).

PAYMENT MODEL
Individual users purchase Zen Pro (subscription), Daily Pass (non-consumable), and Extra Spins (consumables) via Apple In-App Purchase (Guideline 3.1.1). Enterprise customers (companies) purchase seats out-of-app via Stripe at $50/seat/year — a B2B services transaction exempt under Guideline 3.1.3(b). The app contains no buttons, links, or messaging directing individual users to pay outside of IAP.

PRIVACY AND TRACKING
No IDFA, no App Tracking Transparency prompts (we do no tracking), no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We do not read Location, Contacts, Photos, Calendar, or Microphone. HealthKit background delivery is disabled; data is read only when the user opens the Wearable screen and taps Sync.
