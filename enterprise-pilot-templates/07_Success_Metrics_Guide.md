# NeuroQuest Zen Pro — Success Metrics Guide
## What Success Looks Like for Your Pilot

This guide tells you (the HR / People Ops lead) and us (NeuroQuest) exactly 
what we're measuring, what "good" looks like, and when to celebrate. Print 
this and keep it next to your dashboard.

---

## The Five Metrics That Matter

We track five primary success metrics throughout your 75-day pilot. You can 
see all five live in your HR Dashboard at 
`https://neuroquestzen.pro/company-admin`.

### 1. Adoption Rate
> *Are your employees actually signing up?*

**How it's measured:** `(employees who joined) ÷ (seats purchased)`

| Timeframe | Below Expectation | On Track | Excellent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 7 | < 25% | 25–40% | 40%+ |
| Day 14 | < 40% | 40–60% | 60%+ |
| Day 30 | < 55% | 55–75% | 75%+ |
| Day 75 | < 65% | 65–80% | 80%+ |

**If you're below expectation:** the team announcement likely didn't reach 
everyone or didn't land. We'll work with you on a re-launch message.

---

### 2. Active Engagement Rate
> *Of those who joined, who's actually using it?*

**How it's measured:** % of joined employees with at least one session in the 
last 7 days

| Timeframe | Below | On Track | Excellent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 14 | < 30% | 30–50% | 50%+ |
| Day 30 | < 40% | 40–60% | 60%+ |
| Day 75 | < 45% | 45–65% | 65%+ |

**Industry context:** Most corporate wellness apps see 8–15% sustained engagement. 
We aim for 4–5x that. Anything above 40% sustained is genuinely exceptional.

---

### 3. Workforce Resilience Index (WRI)
> *Is your team's mental fitness improving?*

**How it's measured:** A 0–100 score combining mood stability, focus duration, 
and recovery patterns across all active users (anonymized + aggregated). 
You see only the team-wide score, never an individual's.

| WRI Score | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 0–40 | Significant strain — proactive intervention recommended |
| 41–60 | Building resilience — typical starting range |
| 61–75 | Healthy — sustained good performance |
| 76–100 | Thriving — high-performance team state |

**What we expect during the pilot:**
- **Baseline (Day 1–7):** establishes your starting WRI
- **Day 30:** typical lift of +5 to +10 points
- **Day 75:** typical lift of +10 to +18 points

If WRI drops more than 5 points in a 14-day window, your account manager will 
proactively schedule a call.

---

### 4. Burnout Risk Distribution
> *What % of your team is in the danger zone?*

**How it's measured:** Each active user is classified into Low / Moderate / 
High burnout-risk based on our triple-weighted algorithm (see 
`08_How_We_Detect_Burnout.md`). You see only the percentage breakdown, not 
which individuals fall where.

| Risk Tier | Healthy Range | Watch Range | Action Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| **High Risk** | < 10% | 10–18% | 18%+ |
| **Moderate Risk** | < 30% | 30–45% | 45%+ |
| **Low Risk** | > 60% | 45–60% | < 45% |

**Goal across the pilot:** Reduce High-Risk percentage by ≥30% relative to 
baseline. Example: if you start at 15% high-risk, target ≤10.5% by Day 75.

---

### 5. Participation Frequency
> *How often is each user practicing?*

**How it's measured:** Average sessions per active user per week

| Frequency | Meaning |
|---|---|
| < 1 session/week | Awareness only — minimal benefit |
| 1–2 sessions/week | Light practice — measurable mood lift |
| 3–4 sessions/week | **Sweet spot** — meaningful resilience gains |
| 5+ sessions/week | Power users — leading indicators of team momentum |

**Goal:** ≥50% of active users in the 3+ sessions/week tier by Day 30.

## What Success Looks Like at Each Milestone

### Day 7 Snapshot — "Are we launched?"
- ✅ 25%+ of seats claimed
- ✅ At least 5 employees active (so aggregate metrics unlock)
- ✅ Baseline WRI established
- ✅ HR admin has logged into dashboard at least once

### Day 30 Snapshot — "Is it sticking?"
- ✅ 55%+ of seats claimed
- ✅ 40%+ weekly active rate
- ✅ WRI up at least +5 from baseline
- ✅ High-risk burnout tier flat or declining

### Day 75 Snapshot — "Was it worth it?"
- ✅ 65%+ of seats claimed
- ✅ 45%+ sustained weekly active rate
- ✅ WRI up +10 or more from baseline
- ✅ High-risk burnout tier reduced by 30%+
- ✅ Mid-pilot ROI report shows quantified mood/focus lift

---

## What "Pilot Success" Triggers

If your pilot hits **all four** of the Day 75 thresholds above, NeuroQuest 
considers it a clear success and will:

1. Send you a formal **Pilot Outcome Report** suitable for your leadership team
2. Offer a **continuation contract** at the standard $50/seat/year rate
3. Provide a **case-study draft** (only published with your written approval)
4. Include you in our **Founding Customer cohort** (locked-in pricing for 3 years)

---

## What If We Miss Targets?

We don't ghost you. We have a structured response:

| Missed Target | What We Do |
|---|---|
| Adoption < expected at Day 7 | Co-author a re-launch message with you |
| Engagement drops mid-pilot | 30-min diagnostic call + program adjustment |
| WRI declining | Free clinical psychology consult for the program lead |
| Burnout rising despite engagement | Escalation review — root cause may be organizational, not individual |

The pilot is **free and risk-free**. If at Day 75 the data doesn't justify 
continuation, you walk away with a full report and zero obligation.

## How You'll See These Metrics

In your HR Dashboard (`/company-admin`), the **Wellness** tab shows:
- Current WRI with 14-day trend line
- Burnout risk distribution bar
- Adoption rate gauge
- Weekly active rate gauge
- Average sessions per user

NeuroQuest will also send proactive email reports at:
- Day 7 (engagement check)
- Day 14 (first check-in deck)
- Day 30 (one-month review)
- Day 45 (mid-pilot ROI report)
- Day 75 (final outcome report)
