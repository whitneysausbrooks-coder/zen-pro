# NeuroQuest Enterprise Pilot — Sales Kit

Everything you need to land your first 5 enterprise pilots.

| File | When to Use |
|---|---|
| **04_Discovery_Call_Script.md** | Before / during the prospect's first Zoom call |
| **01_Pilot_Agreement.md** | Send within 1 hour of a verbal yes (use Docusign Free or HelloSign) |
| **02_Welcome_Email.md** | Send within 1 hour of payment landing in Stripe |
| **03_Team_Announcement.md** | Attach to the welcome email — the buyer sends this to their employees |

## Recommended Day-of Workflow

1. **Take the discovery call** (use script #4)
2. **End the call with a verbal yes**
3. **Within 1 hour:** send signed pilot agreement (#1) + Stripe invoice
4. **Within 24 hrs:** customer pays
5. **Within 1 hour of payment:** send welcome email (#2) with team announcement (#3) attached
6. **Day 7:** first engagement report
7. **Day 14:** check-in call

## What to Customize Before First Use

- Your state in Section 9 of the Pilot Agreement (governing law)
- The 8-character invite code in Welcome Email (generate per company in admin dashboard)
- The App Store link once your iOS app is approved
- Your direct phone number if you want to give it out for "URGENT" matters
