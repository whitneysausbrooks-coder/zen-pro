## NeuroQuest + Your Wearable

NeuroQuest works with the device you already own. Connecting it is **completely optional**, but it makes your personal Resilience Score dramatically more accurate — and gives you 4–8 weeks of early warning before stress turns into burnout.

**You stay in control. Your individual data is never visible to your employer or HR.**

---

## What we read (and only this)

| Signal | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Heart Rate Variability (HRV)** | The single best indicator of how recovered your nervous system is |
| **Sleep duration** | The largest lever on next-day mood, focus, and resilience |
| **Daily steps / activity** | Light movement supports stress recovery |

We do **not** read: location, GPS, messages, contacts, workouts you haven't logged, calendar, or any health record beyond the three signals above.

---

## Compatible devices

| Brand | How to connect |
|---|---|
| **Apple Watch** (any model) | Open the NeuroQuest mobile app → Profile → Connect → Apple Health → tap "Allow" |
| **Fitbit** (any model) | NeuroQuest app or web → Profile → Connect → Fitbit → sign in to Fitbit |
| **Garmin** (any model) | Profile → Connect → Garmin → sign in to Garmin Connect |
| **WHOOP** | Profile → Connect → WHOOP → sign in to WHOOP |
| **Oura Ring** | Profile → Connect → Oura → sign in to Oura |
| **Android phone or wearable** | Profile → Connect → Google Fit → sign in to your Google account |
| **No wearable?** | You're still in. Just pick "Manual entry" — you'll log sleep and energy in 10 seconds a day |

---

## How long it takes

- **First-time connection:** about 90 seconds
- **Daily effort after that:** zero. Data syncs in the background while you sleep, work, and live your life.

---

## What you'll see in your personal dashboard

Once connected, within 24 hours you'll see:

- Your **Neuro-Resilience Score** (0–100)
- A 14-day trend of how you're recovering
- Personalized recommendations — sometimes a 2-minute breath exercise, sometimes a reminder to step away from your screen
- Your private mood + focus history

---

## What HR sees

- A team-wide resilience trend, **only** when 5 or more colleagues are participating
- A burnout-risk distribution (e.g. "70% Low / 25% Moderate / 5% High")
- **Never** your name, your score, your data, or anything traceable to you

This privacy floor is enforced at the database — not by promise.

## Frequently asked

**Q: What if I take my watch off for a few days?**
A: That's fine. The score uses a 7-day rolling average and gracefully handles gaps.

**Q: Can I disconnect later?**
A: Yes — Profile → Connections → Disconnect. Your historical data is also deletable on request.

**Q: Does this drain my battery?**
A: No. We use the same passive sync your fitness app already does.

**Q: Will my doctor/insurer/manager see this?**
A: No. Ever. Period.

**Q: I have multiple devices — which should I connect?**
A: Pick the one you wear most consistently. Apple Watch + iPhone is the most common setup; the Oura Ring is the most precise for sleep.
