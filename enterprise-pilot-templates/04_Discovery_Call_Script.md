# Discovery Call Script — 15–30 Minutes

> **Goal of the call:** Confirm fit, get buying authority, set the next step 
> (signed agreement + invoice) within 24 hours. This is not a pitch — it's a 
> diagnosis.

---

## Opening (2 min)

> "Thanks for making the time, [Name]. Before I show you anything, I'd love to 
> understand what's actually going on with your team — that way I can tell you 
> honestly whether NeuroQuest is the right fit or not. Sound good?"

---

## Discovery Questions — Ask These in Order (10 min)

### 1. Why now?
> "What made you reach out about employee wellness specifically right now?"

🎯 **Listen for:** burnout incident, exec mandate, attrition spike, board pressure, 
upcoming benefits-renewal cycle. Their answer becomes your closing language.

### 2. What have you tried?
> "What have you already tried — meditation apps, EAP, wellness stipends? 
> What worked, what didn't?"

🎯 **Listen for:** low engagement, "everyone signs up and no one uses it," 
no measurable outcome.

### 3. How do you measure success today?
> "If we did this together and 90 days from now it was a clear win — what 
> would have changed? Engagement scores? Sick days? Retention?"

🎯 **Listen for:** specific numbers. If they have none, help them define one.

### 4. Who decides?
> "Who else is involved in deciding to move forward? Is there a budget owner 
> or a contract that needs to go through procurement?"

🎯 **Critical question.** No clear decision-maker = no deal.

### 5. Timeline & seats
> "If we agreed today this was the right next step, when would you want to 
> launch — and roughly how many people in this first cohort?"

🎯 **Recommend 25–100 seats for first pilot.** Smaller than 25 = not enough 
data. Larger than 100 = harder to white-glove.

### 6. Tech requirements
> "Anything we need to know about your tech setup — SSO requirements, IT 
> review, security questionnaire?"

🎯 **For pilots ≤ 100 seats, SSO is usually optional.** For 100+ or regulated 
industries (healthcare, finance), SSO and security review are required — 
add 2 weeks to the timeline.

---

## Show NeuroQuest (5 min)

Share your screen. Walk through:

1. **The employee experience** (90 sec) — open the iOS app or web, do one 
   game, show the NeuroResilience Score
2. **The admin dashboard** (90 sec) — show team metrics, burnout heatmap, 
   ROI panel
3. **The charity angle** (60 sec) — Compassion Wheel + 30% donation 
   transparency

> "I want to be clear — this isn't another mindfulness app. The science 
> behind the NeuroResilience Score is HRV-, sleep-, and activity-based. We 
> measure what we change."

---

## The Ask (3 min)

> "Based on what you've told me, here's what I'd propose:
>
> **A 75-day pilot with [X] seats — fully complimentary, no invoice, no credit card.** 
> If your team uses it and your dashboard shows real engagement, you have the 
> option to continue at $50 per seat per year. If it doesn't move the needle, 
> we shake hands and walk away — zero cost, zero obligation.
>
> The reason I'm offering it free is I'd rather earn the renewal with real data 
> than charge you to find out. **What would have to be true for you to say 
> yes today?**"

---

## Close (2 min)

If yes → **"Great. I'll send you a one-page agreement within the hour — 
no invoice, no card. Once you sign, your team is live within 60 minutes. 
What email should I send everything to?"**

If "I need to think about it" → **"Totally understand. What's the question 
that's still open in your head?"** Address it. If still no, schedule a 
specific follow-up date.

If no → **"Appreciate your honesty. Mind if I ask — what would have made 
this a fit?"** Their answer is gold for your next 10 calls.

---

## After the Call (within 1 hour)

1. ✉️ Send: signed-by-you pilot agreement (Docusign) + Stripe invoice
2. 📅 Schedule: Day 14 check-in already on the calendar
3. 📝 Log: their answers to the 6 discovery questions in your CRM (or a Notes doc)

## Pricing Cheat Sheet

**All pilots are 75 days, fully complimentary.** Pricing only applies 
**after** the pilot if the customer chooses to continue.

| Seats | Pilot Cost | Annual Cost (post-pilot, $50/seat) |
|---|---|---|
| 25 seats | **$0** | $1,250/yr |
| 50 seats | **$0** | $2,500/yr |
| 100 seats | **$0** | $5,000/yr |
| 250 seats | **$0** | $12,500/yr |
| 500+ seats | **$0** | Custom |

### Why free?

- Removes the procurement / credit-card friction that kills 80% of B2B pilot deals
- Forces us to earn the renewal with real engagement data, not a sunk-cost contract
- Generates testimonials, case studies, and reference customers faster
- Caps risk: max liability per signed agreement is $100

### Guardrails to protect your time

- **Cap pilots at 5 active at one time** — beyond that, white-glove quality drops
- **Require named decision-maker** on the call who can sign the conversion contract
- **Require minimum 25 seats** — anything smaller doesn't generate meaningful data
- **75 days is firm** — extensions only granted with a signed conversion agreement
