# NeuroQuest Zen Pro — HR Admin Quick Start Guide
## For the HR / People Ops Lead at Your Company

Welcome to NeuroQuest Zen Pro Enterprise. This guide walks you through 
everything you need to roll the platform out to your team in under 15 minutes 
of setup time.

---

## What You'll Have After Setup

- A private **HR Dashboard** to monitor your team's wellness program
- A **company invite code** your employees will use to join
- Visibility into seats used, days remaining in your pilot, and aggregate 
  wellness trends — without ever seeing any individual employee's personal data

---

## Step 1 — Your HR Dashboard (5 minutes)

### Where to go

> ## 🌐 https://neuroquestzen.pro/company-admin

Open this URL on your laptop or phone. We recommend bookmarking it.

### How to log in

You'll see two fields:

1. **Your admin email** — the email address NeuroQuest set up for you during 
   onboarding. It's in your welcome email under "Sign in with email."
2. **Company code** — an 8-character code, like `YM8KE7SW`. Same code your 
   employees will use to join.

Click **Sign In**. The browser will remember you on this device.

### What you'll see inside

The dashboard has three tabs:

| Tab | What's there |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Seats used vs purchased, days remaining in your pilot, your invite code with a one-click "Copy invite message" button |
| **Team** | Every employee who has joined — their email, department, join date, and activity count. You can remove employees from here |
| **Wellness** | Aggregate metrics: participation rate, average mood, engagement, and a 14-day trend chart |

### Important: Privacy by Design

- You can see **aggregate scores only** — never any individual employee's mood, 
  burnout risk, or engagement number
- Aggregate metrics are **suppressed entirely until 5 or more employees** are 
  active. This prevents anyone from reverse-engineering who is or isn't doing well
- Only the employee themselves sees their personal scores in the app
- Every action you take in the dashboard is logged to a tamper-evident audit log

This isn't a limitation — it's the design. NeuroQuest gives you organizational 
health insight without surveilling individuals. Share this section with your 
legal or HR compliance team if needed.

## Step 2 — Inviting Your Team (5 minutes)

### Option A — The fast way (recommended)

1. In your HR Dashboard, go to the **Overview** tab
2. Click **"Copy invite message"** under your company code
3. Paste into a company-wide email or Slack channel
4. Done

The copied message will look like:

> Join NeuroQuest at https://neuroquestzen.pro/join — Code: YM8KE7SW

### Option B — Custom announcement

If you want to write your own intro, NeuroQuest provided a `Team Announcement` 
template in your welcome email package. Customize it with your company tone and 
send. Just make sure it includes both:

- The join URL: **https://neuroquestzen.pro/join**
- Your company code: **(the 8-character code)**

### What employees do (so you can answer questions)

1. They visit `https://neuroquestzen.pro/join`
2. They enter your company code
3. They confirm the company name they see
4. They enter their work email, name, and (optionally) department
5. They're in. They pick a wellness game and start a 5-minute daily session

There's no IT setup. No password forced on them. No app install required (web 
works on any device; iOS app coming soon).

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I see which specific employees are at burnout risk?** 
No. By design, NeuroQuest never shows you individual scores. You see only team 
averages, and only when 5+ employees are active. Your employees see their own 
data inside the app.

**Q: An employee left the company. How do I remove them?** 
Open the **Team** tab, find their row, click **Remove**. Done immediately.

**Q: I forgot my login.** 
Your admin email is fixed at the email NeuroQuest used during onboarding. If 
you've lost it, email **admin@neuroquestllc.info** and we'll restore your access.

**Q: Can I add another HR teammate as an admin?** 
Currently each company has one HR admin email. To change it or add a second, 
email **admin@neuroquestllc.info**.

**Q: Can my team use this on their phones?** 
Yes — the web app works on any phone browser. The iOS app is in App Store 
review and Android arrives Q3 2026.

**Q: What happens to our data after the pilot ends?** 
If you don't continue, your data is retained for 30 days in case you change 
your mind, then deleted on request. You can also export everything to CSV from 
your dashboard before that.

**Q: What if I want to add more seats during the pilot?** 
Email **admin@neuroquestllc.info** with the new seat count. We'll bump it the 
same day, no charge during the free pilot.
